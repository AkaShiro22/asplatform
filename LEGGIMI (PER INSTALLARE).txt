How to install Voxis Platform on your host:
1. Open config.php via text editor.
2. Edit all the needed fields and save.
3. Upload voxis_platform folder to your hosting.
4. Open your browser and navigate to http://hosting/voxis_platform/install.php
5. Delete install.php file.
6. Add the following cron job to your server's crontab: */3 * * * * /usr/bin/curl --silent http://hosting/voxis_platform/cronjob.php
7. Enjoy!
